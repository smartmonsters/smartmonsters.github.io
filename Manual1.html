Registering the name of a new player character in the blockchain temporarily locks 20 coins. For testnet, these can be created easily by the built-in miner. Mined coins must mature for 120 blocks before they can be used.


Starting a new character

Click "New" button, and choose a name and a color. Creating a new character is a transaction -- after it has confirmed in the blockchain, the new char appears on the map. Each color has its own base in one of the map corners.

Yellow Upper left corner
Red Upper right corner
Green Lower right corner
Blue Lower left corner

The center and the 4 color team bases are safe zones, combat is not possible here. Players of other colors can't walk across the base perimeter (the tiles marked on map are still within the safe zone).



Movement in safe zones

To move, set a path to any destination with a mouse click or ctrl-click to specify individual waypoints of the path, then press "Go" button. Moving is a transaction -- the path will change color from grey to purple after it has confirmed in the blockchain.

Four 2-way teleporters connect the center and the bases. To teleport, stand still on the bright glowing tile.




Strategic movement orders (works everywhere)

If a character stands still and then get new waypoints, and the last waypoint (end point of path) is within 12 tiles distance to a flag pole, this area becomes the ordered target area for next round.
(1 round == 2000 blocks)

If the order was given less than 1000 blocks before the old round ended, it may not be effective for the new round, and will only go into effect 1 round later. (500 blocks = 50% chance, 250 blocks = 25% chance, and so on). This is to balance the disadvantage of sending your move before knowing other player's moves.


Movement outside safezones

It is not possible to "manually" step outside of a safe zone.

Movement orders to battlefield areas are irrevocable. This sort of movement, and fights between characters are always AI controlled.

Combat results depend only on character equipment and tactical situation.

Basic Strategy

It makes sense to visit the center first and get some free items, most notably the Amulet of Word of Recall. Then set path to one of the coins-spawning areas. Choose wisely: once the characters leave safe zone, they are on their own and players can't be allowed to babysit them. The point of this game is really to predict the outcome of battles and to send your guys to fight on the winning side, preferably where the losing side is at risk of annihilation rather than mere rout.

When a new block is received, the node compiles a list with the queued Strategic Movement Orders of all players. It is most informative around mid-round, when 1000 blocks are left until a new round begins, and the chance of newly submitted (strategic) moves to be active next round is starting to drop from 100%. Which coin spawning area is not under attack and also not overcrowded?

Going into the combat zone from the map center is more dangerous than from your own base, easy to get sandwiched between groups of hostiles. However, when a new round begins, all characters of the same color (both PCs and monsters) get a 100 block time window to leave the center towards their own sector of the combat zone, making it slightly less dangerous (Other colors will automatically wait, and then get 20-block-timewindows per color so that everyone can leave the center in the direction they want.
