Huntercoin-ish vocabulary


SynonymsDefinition
Playernameaddress Name Address When a new Player Character (who has a new, unique name) is created, an amount of coins is locked under this address, and a data field of 1 constant string and 1 variable string ("Name", "Value") is created in the blockchain, containing the PC's name, and all moves of that player.
General Player,
Hunter		 One of the aforementioned Player Characters with unique names.
Heart Soldier Character,
Hunter		 Additional characters can be gained by running over a heart. These chars don't have unique names and don't need coins to be created.
Heart Now spawning semi-randomly near coins, and only in combat zones where movement is automatic
(so no one can farm them manually or with a bot)
Transfer Generals can be transferred to a new address, taking all their Heart Soldiers with them.
Useful for sending PCs to a new wallet.
Color Team,
Faction		 All characters are either on yellow, red, green or blue.
There is generally no team damage (can't hurt members of same color, not even monsters)
In combat zones, all characters will now automatically attack other colors, or flee from them if outmatched.
Bank Spawnstrip Banking means stepping on one of the colored tiles in the 4 map corners.
This will send all looted coins to the wallet (i.e. player's name address or reward address) and the PC's character level is reset to 1.
Reward addresses can be set with "Config..." button.
Gamestate Cached data, extracted from the node's blockchain and always kept in memory.
Snapshots of the gamestate are saved in "game.dat", if this file is deleted, the node will regenerate it by replaying the blockchain.
In case of a blockchain re-org, an older gamestate snapshot is fetched and updated by reading all newer blocks.

Gamestates never communicate with gamestates of other nodes, all information they get comes from their own node's blockchain.
